Cozie Apple

Cozie Apple - an iOS app for human comfort data collection.

Coming soon!Contact us

Free and Easy to Use

Building occupants can complete a right-here-right now survey directly from their Apple watch. Without the need of having to open an app in their Phone or a survey link.

Open Source

Cozie Apple is an Open Source project and together with Cozie Fitbit, allows researchers to focus on the data collection. We have taken care of all the programming for you!

Powered by Apple ResearchKit

Cozie Apple iOS app uses Apple's Research Kit. A software framework for Apple apps that let researchers gather robust and meaningful data.
